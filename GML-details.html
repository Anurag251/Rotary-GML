July: Transition Month

Launching First Issue of GML - July 2023

By Raj Shrestha

Editor / Chair - Distric Publication and GML Sub-Committee 2023-24

Read More Download PDF

Hurray! I'm absolutely thrilled to announce the launch of the Governor's Monthly Letter (GML) for the Rotary Year 2023-24. It's an incaredible pleasure to present this first issue to all of you.

We've gone all out and revamped the entire structure of the GML, ensuring a completely refreshed content experience. You'll be delighted to find an array of reading materials, captivating visual presentations, and convenient QR links to access various accitiona resources.

I encourage each and every one of you to dive into the pages of the GML, explore the content. and take the opportunity to learn. share and comment. making this community even more vibrant. I want to thank DG Rajendra Prasad Dhoju for his support and extend a big round of applause to the GML Publishing Team for their unwavering support and hard work, putting in extra hours to make this publication look absolutely beautiful. Your efforts are truly appreciated!

Let's raise a toast to this successful launch and to an exciting Rotary Year ahead. Here's to the GML, the readers, and the incredible team behind it - may it be a tremendous success and bring immense value to everyone involved. Cheers!!
